About Me

My name is Christopher Hylton, but I go by CJ. I am 22 years old. I was born in Colorado, where I currently live, but take classes at Brigham Young University - Idaho. My semester schedule is Winter/Spring, and I enjoy the classes and the campus while I am up there. I have 2 wonderful, loving parents, as well as a younger sister, who is 20.

I spend my time doing a few things outside of school. One thing I very much enjoy doing is playing the game Pokemon Go, which is developed by Niantic Labs. I started playing it a little over a year ago, on my birthday, and since that day, I have walked around 3561km, or around 2,212 miles.
