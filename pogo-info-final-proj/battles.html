Gym Battles

Gyms, as mentioned previously, are giant structures that appear across the Pokemon Go landscape. In addition to having the PokeStop disc that give items, they can also be used to house Pokemon from various trainers. Depending on what team you are, whether you are Instinct, Mystic, or Valor, you can either fight the Pokemon in the Gyms or put your own in to defend them. This is dependent on whether the Gym is the of the same team you are.

If the gym you are at is your team's alignment, you may place a Pokemon inside it to defend it. Placing Pokemon in the gyms to defend them will earn you Pokecoins, which can be used in the in-game shop to buy items. Trainers can earn a max of 50 coins a day, and the maximum coins comes from around 8.5 hours of total gym defense. There is a limit of 6 pokemon allowed in each gym, but there is no limit to the amount of Gyms you can place Pokemon in.

However, if you are a member of an opposing team, you can battle against the Pokemon inside the gym to knock them out and put your own Pokemon inside. Be sure to bring powerful pokemon that are the correct counters to what you will be battling, for best results!

Raids

Raids are a kind of Gym Battles in Pokemon Go. There are 4 main types, each with their corresponding egg and star rating. They are as follows: 1-star. 3-star. 4-star(Mega Raids), and 5-star(Legendary Raids). 1-star raids are the easiest, and 5-star legendary raids are the most difficult. Legendary raids are the most common ways to obtain Legendary Pokemon, which are usually very powerful Pokemon.

Trainers are able to participate in raids using one of two items, daily Raid Passes or Premium Battle Passes. Once per day, you can receive a free raid pass from spinning the photo disc of a Gym. Additional Premium Battle Passes can be bought in the Pokemon Go Store with Pokecoins. Premium Battle Passes can also be obtained by completing certain timed and special research.

PVP (Player Verses Player)

Player verses Player gameplay, usually referred to as PVP, is the online battling environment where you can battle against other Trainers around the world to be the very best! Completion of these battles will earn you various rewards, including stardust, TMs (used to teach your Pokemon different attacks), and encounters with various pokemon. These encounters will have boosted IVs, increasing the chances of perfect stat Pokemon.

DISCLAIMER! PVP is incredibly rewarding, but tends to have a steep learning curve and requires lots of resources including stardust, candy, and patience. Competitive battling can be discouraging, but it is well worth the effort in the long run.
