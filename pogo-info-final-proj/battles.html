Raids

Raids are the first kinds of battles in Pokemon Go. There are 4 main types, each with their corresponding egg and star rating. They are as follows: 1-star. 3-star. 4-star(Mega Raids), and 5-star(Legendary Raids). 1-star raids are the easiest, and 5-star legendary raids are the most difficult. Legendary raids are the most common ways to obtain Legendary Pokemon, which are usually very powerful Pokemon.

Trainers are able to participate in raids using one of two items, daily Raid Passes or Premium Battle Passes. Once per day, you can receive a free raid pass from spinning the photo disc of a Gym. Additional Premium Battle Passes can be bought in the Pokemon Go Store with Pokecoins. Premium Battle Passes can also be obtained by completing certain timed and special research.
